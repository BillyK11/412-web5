Wholesale Repairs

Contact us to find out more about doing wholesale dental handpiece repair.

As Canada's largest independent Dental handpiece repair company we strive to cover Dentist nationwide but need others to get there. We offer wholesale repairs to any company that services the dental industry. We understand that fixing dental handpieces can be a complicated thing for many indepandant smaller companies. Finding parts, tooling and schematics can be a very time consuming and costly. We are here to help. We can offer fast turn around times, competitive pricing and great warranties. Most highspeeds will have 6 months warranty on the bearings and 1 year on the chuck. We are Kavo, Star Dental, and Morita certified.
